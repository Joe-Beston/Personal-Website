About Me

Greetings! My name is Joe Beston. I am 22 years old, originally from Naperville, IL. I joined the United States Air Force after completing high school and trained to become an Operations Intelligence Analyst. Currently, I am a Staff Sergeant and serve part-time for the Iowa Air National Guard while pursuing my B.B.A. in Business Analytics & Information Systems at the University of Iowa.

My passion lies in the realm of data analytics, where I am driven to transform raw data into timely, accessible, and useful information and insights. I believe that data has the power to improve decision-making and drive better outcomes. My academic coursework has been instrumental in teaching me the newest analytical tools and techniques, while my role as an Operations Intelligence Analyst offered me the opportunity to apply them in practical situations. It is truly rewarding to see how the insights generated from data can bring positive change to whatever I apply it to, and I am committed to continuously learning and expanding my skills to contribute to this endeavor.
